ABOUT

Krishna Chaitanya junior college was established in 2006 with an aim to provide Quality education to the students of Nellore. With its suspendous success and repeated request from the student Community and enthusiasm parents have prompted them to start a Residential campus. The institution started Residential junior College exclusively for Boys in Wahabpet (prabhanjana campus) and Residential junior college exclusively for girls in wahabpet (vasishta campus). Later with an ardent view the institution was expanded in different areas. Since then it has been attracting the students. Every Care is taken to ensure students success in Intermediate and competitive exams like IIT, JEE, AIEEE, BITSAT & NEET both at the same time. Certain teaching procedure are moulded considering the standard of students and their goals.

Our Academic System :

The academic council of the college consists of compartments Heads and academic supervisors with the principal as the team leader. The council meets every team to plan our strategies for better performance of the teachers, materials, infra-structure and program for effective methodology. The whole years plan concerning academics are analyzed, discussed and finalized in the Academic council. Here Knowledge imparting will not help the students to achieve the ultimate goal. Hence the college has deviced many systems to equip the students to achieve their ambition. Our Uniqueness is weekly unit test i.e., Every tuesday and saturday, unit tests are conducted in the first two periods of forenoon session and afternoon session. Students get ample oppurtunities for learning this weekly lessons then and there and prepare well for final examinations.
