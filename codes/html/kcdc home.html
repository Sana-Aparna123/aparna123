WELCOME TO KRISHNA CHAITANYA GROUP OF
EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS



Krishna Chaitanya College has carved a niche for itself over a period of two decades. The milestone for Krishna Chaitanya College was laid by a two young men Dr.RV Krishna Reddy and P.Chandra Sekhar Reddy. In the Year 1998, Krishna Chaitanya College was started with three departments, 140 students and limited faculty in Nellore, Andhra Pradesh. Since then it is constantly progressing in motivating students and staff engaged in active learning through the best use of modern technology. Krishna Chaitanya College is not just an educational institution, it is a commitment, towards building a new generation of smart and capable individuals. It not only provides quality Education, but teaches them the right attitude towards life.

One among the top colleges in our state. Krishna Chaitanya College

has wide reputation for holistic approach towards grooming the students. Today, Krishna Chaitanya happens to be one of the largest educational groups in the state of Andhra Pradesh with more than 300 faculty members and around 200 administrative staff and above 10000 students. Krishna Chaitanya is known for establishing unique traditions in every aspects of its functioning.

DIRECTOR'S DESK:

It is a matter of great pleasure for us to welcome you to Krishna Chaitanya Colleges
on behalf of the faculty members and supporting staff. Our college is well known for
its well equipped laboratories, good library facilities, highly qualified and dedicated
teachers and conducive atmosphere for teaching and learning. Professors from
reputed institutions quite often visit the college to give invited talks, lectures and
seminars. We also organize training programmers and conferences on regional and
national level with the financial support of bodies. These programs facilitate excellent
means to enrich your learning outside the class rooms. We believe that our graduates
and post graduates need technological knowledge, communication and problem
solving skills in order to compete in the present global scenario. We are confident that
preparing today’s youth for future success requires a team effort. Therefore the
college and its all stake holders must work together for quality assurance in
education.
