DIRECTOR'S DESK:

It is a matter of great pleasure for us to welcome you to Krishna Chaitanya Colleges
on behalf of the faculty members and supporting staff. Our college is well known for
its well equipped laboratories, good library facilities, highly qualified and dedicated
teachers and conducive atmosphere for teaching and learning. Professors from
reputed institutions quite often visit the college to give invited talks, lectures and
seminars. We also organize training programmers and conferences on regional and
national level with the financial support of bodies. These programs facilitate excellent
means to enrich your learning outside the class rooms. We believe that our graduates
and post graduates need technological knowledge, communication and problem
solving skills in order to compete in the present global scenario. We are confident that
preparing today’s youth for future success requires a team effort. Therefore the
college and its all stake holders must work together for quality assurance in
education.
